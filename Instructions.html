Controls and Instructions

Controls

You, the player can control the plane by using the "Up Arrow","W" or the "Spacebar" key on your keyboard :)


Instructions

In this game, you take the role of a pilot tasked with navigating a city's skyline.

The ultimate mission of the player is to steer clear of the towering buildings to prevent a disastrous crash.

Every building that the player successfully avoids earns them points, which can be used to flex on your friends!
